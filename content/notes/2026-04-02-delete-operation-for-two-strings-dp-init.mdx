<p>其实做动态规划相关题目的朋友难免会碰到该如何初始化 dp 数组的问题&#xff0c;有的解答是 dp[i]代表的是 (i-1)&#xff0c;有时候又代表的是 i&#xff0c;那到底是应该用哪种呢&#xff1f;这两种又有什么区别&#xff1f;今天这道题带你整明白嘿嘿&#x1f60e;</p> 
<h2>题目描述</h2> 
<h2>英文版描述</h2> 
<p>Given two strings <code>word1</code> and <code>word2</code>, return <em>the minimum number of </em><strong><em>steps</em></strong><em> required to make</em><code>word1</code><em>and</em><code>word2</code><em>the same</em>.</p> 
<p>In one <strong>step</strong>, you can delete exactly one character in either string.</p> 
<p><strong>Example 1:</strong></p> 
<p><strong>Input:</strong> word1 &#61; &#34;sea&#34;, word2 &#61; &#34;eat&#34; <strong>Output:</strong> 2 <strong>Explanation:</strong> You need one step to make &#34;sea&#34; to &#34;ea&#34; and another step to make &#34;eat&#34; to &#34;ea&#34;.</p> 
<p><strong>Example 2:</strong></p> 
<p><strong>Input:</strong> word1 &#61; &#34;leetcode&#34;, word2 &#61; &#34;etco&#34; <strong>Output:</strong> 4</p> 
<p><strong>Constraints:</strong></p> 
<ul><li><code>1 <&#61; word1.length, word2.length <&#61; 500</code></li><li><code>word1</code> and <code>word2</code> consist of only lowercase English letters.</li></ul>

<h2>总结</h2> 
<p>这两种写法的主要差别其实就是在初始化的时候&#xff0c;可以看下他俩的 dp 数组</p> 
<p>解法 1 的动规数组的含义是word1 取 [0, i] 范围内并且word2取[0, j] 的范围内时&#xff0c;使得 word1 和 word2 相同所需的最小步数是dp[i][j] &#xff0c;而解法 2 的动规数组的含义是word1 取 [0, i-1] 范围内并且word2取[0, j-1] 的范围内时&#xff0c;使得 word1 和 word2 相同所需的最小步数是dp[i][j]</p>
